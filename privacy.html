Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What NextUp Is

NextUp Routine Clock is a free visual schedule tool designed to help children understand their daily routines. It was created as a student project at Portland State University.

Data Stored on Your Device

All schedule data, activity names, times, icons, and uploaded photos are stored locally on your device using your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device. We cannot see it, access it, or retrieve it. If you clear your browser data, it is gone.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how people use NextUp. This collects anonymous, aggregated data such as how many people visit the site, what devices and browsers they use, and which pages they view. This helps us improve the app.

Google Analytics does not collect your name, email, schedule data, or any personal information. You can learn more about how Google handles this data at Google's Privacy Policy.

What We Do Not Collect

We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information. We do not collect your child's schedule, activity data, or uploaded photos. We do not use cookies for advertising. We do not sell or share any data with third parties.

Children's Privacy

NextUp is designed to be used by parents and caregivers on behalf of their children. The app does not knowingly collect any information from children. All setup and configuration is intended to be done by an adult.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us through the feedback form.
